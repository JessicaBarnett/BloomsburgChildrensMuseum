Host your Birthday Party at BCM!

Make your child's birthday exciting and memorable. Let us handle the cleaning!

Basic Packages include:

  • 90 minute use of our party room, including a kitchenette, tables and chairs.

  • Full day-long access to the museum

  • Free admission for all Adults attending the party

  • Invitiations and nametags for the kids

  • A helpful party host who will help set up 30 minutes prior to the party, and will clean up afterwards.

Plus Packages include all the above, plus:

  • Pizza & Cake provided by our food partners, Atillio's Pizzaria and Aversa's Bakery.

  • A visit from Bloomer, the Museum's Mascot, or a Museum Entertainer of your choice

  • paper goods, decorations, and party favors

Basic packages start at $250, Plus packages at $320. Includes admission for up to 15 children. (Additional children are $10 each, with a maximum of 20.) We ask that there be at least 1 adult present for every 4 children

members recieve a 15% discount on the total price
