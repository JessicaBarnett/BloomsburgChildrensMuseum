Join the Museum Family!

As a valued supporter of the Museum, you'll receive:

  • 50% discounted admission price all year

  • 10% discounted admission for up to 4 guests

  • 15% off the price of birthday parties and programs.

  • 10% off in the museum store

  • 10% off parking at local garages

  • 10% discount at affiliated organizations, including the Bloomsburg Aquarium.

  • Invitations to all of our kid-friendly members-only events.

Join Now!

Our Mission at the Museum is to educate and enrich the lives of kids of all backgrounds, in Pennsylvania and surrounding areas. As a supporter, you are helping us reach that goal by providing scholarships and afterschool programs for kids from lower-income families, as well as helping us continue the work we do every day to delight and teach the kids who visit us.

As a member, you will also be joining a vibrant community of parents, families, and educators. We would be thrilled to meet you at our biannual members' social, the Spring Member's Picnic, the Fall Carnival, or any of our members-only events! You'll also receive a free subscription to the Museum's quarterly publication, the Bloomer News.

Dues are only $10 per month. All member dues to the museum are tax-deductible.
